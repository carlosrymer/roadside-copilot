# Roadside Assistance Policy — Basic Plan

**Policy form:** RA-BASIC-2026
**Underwriter:** Meridian Auto Mutual

## 1. Definitions

1.1 "Covered Vehicle" means the private passenger automobile listed on the
declarations page, registered and licensed for road use.

1.2 "Disablement" means the Covered Vehicle is unable to be driven safely due to
mechanical failure, electrical failure, flat tire, dead battery, or lack of fuel.

1.3 "Service Call" means a single dispatch of a service provider to the Covered
Vehicle's location.

## 2. Eligibility

2.1 Coverage applies only while the policy is active and premiums are current.

2.2 The driver at the time of Disablement must be the policyholder or a listed
driver, or operating the Covered Vehicle with permission.

## 3. Covered Services

3.1 **Towing.** When the Covered Vehicle cannot be made driveable at the
roadside, it will be towed to the nearest qualified repair facility.

3.2 **Battery & Jump-Start.** On-site battery jump-start. If the battery cannot
hold a charge, towing under §3.1 applies.

3.3 **Flat Tire.** On-site replacement of a flat tire with the member's
serviceable spare. If no serviceable spare is available, towing under §3.1
applies.

3.4 **Fuel & Fluid Delivery.** Delivery of fuel to reach the nearest station. The
member pays for the fuel delivered.

## 4. Limits

4.1 **Service Call Limit.** Up to **two (2)** Service Calls per policy year.

4.2 **Towing Distance.** Towing is covered up to **15 miles** per Disablement.
Mileage beyond 15 miles is billed to the member at the prevailing per-mile rate.

4.3 **Benefit Cap.** Covered services are subject to a **$75 per Service Call**
benefit cap; amounts above the cap are the member's responsibility.

## 5. Exclusions

The following are **not** covered:

5.1 Lockout assistance. (Available on Standard and Premium plans.)

5.2 Winching or extraction of any kind. (Available on the Premium plan only.)

5.3 Collision recovery and towing of a vehicle damaged in a collision.
Accident-related towing is covered under the vehicle's collision coverage, not
this roadside plan. (Collision recovery is available on Standard and Premium
plans.)

5.4 Vehicles used for commercial purposes, ride-hail, or delivery at the time of
Disablement.

5.5 Disablement occurring during racing, off-road driving, or on non-maintained
terrain.

5.6 Disablement while the driver is operating under the influence of alcohol or
drugs.

5.7 Vehicles not registered or not licensed for road use.

## 6. Additional Benefits

6.1 No trip interruption, rental, taxi, lockout, winching, or collision benefits
are included on the Basic plan.
