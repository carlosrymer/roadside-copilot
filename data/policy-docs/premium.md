# Roadside Assistance Policy — Premium Plan

**Policy form:** RA-PREMIUM-2026
**Underwriter:** Meridian Auto Mutual

## 1. Definitions

1.1 "Covered Vehicle" means the private passenger automobile listed on the
declarations page, registered and licensed for road use.

1.2 "Disablement" means the Covered Vehicle is unable to be driven safely due to
mechanical failure, electrical failure, flat tire, dead battery, lockout, lack of
fuel, or collision damage.

1.3 "Service Call" means a single dispatch of a service provider to the Covered
Vehicle's location.

## 2. Eligibility

2.1 Coverage applies only while the policy is active and premiums are current.

2.2 The driver at the time of Disablement must be the policyholder or a listed
driver, or operating the Covered Vehicle with permission.

## 3. Covered Services

3.1 **Towing.** When the Covered Vehicle cannot be made driveable at the
roadside, it will be towed to the nearest qualified repair facility, or to a
facility of the member's choice within the distance limit in §4.2.

3.2 **Battery & Jump-Start.** On-site battery jump-start. If the battery cannot
hold a charge, towing under §3.1 applies.

3.3 **Flat Tire.** On-site replacement of a flat tire with the member's
serviceable spare. If no serviceable spare is available, towing under §3.1
applies.

3.4 **Fuel & Fluid Delivery.** Delivery of enough fuel or fluids to reach the
nearest station. Cost of fuel/fluids is included under this plan.

3.5 **Lockout.** On-site assistance to gain entry when keys are lost, broken, or
locked inside the Covered Vehicle.

3.6 **Winching / Extraction.** Extraction when the Covered Vehicle is stuck
within 100 feet of a paved or maintained road. Recovery from off-road or
non-maintained terrain is excluded under §5.

3.7 **Collision Recovery.** Following a collision, towing under §3.1 is covered
regardless of fault, including recovery of a non-driveable vehicle.

## 4. Limits

4.1 **Service Call Limit.** Up to **six (6)** Service Calls per policy year.

4.2 **Towing Distance.** Towing is covered up to **100 miles** per Disablement.
Mileage beyond 100 miles is billed to the member at the prevailing per-mile rate.

4.3 **Benefit Cap.** No per-call dollar cap applies to covered services within
the distance and call limits above.

## 5. Exclusions

The following are **not** covered:

5.1 Vehicles used for commercial purposes, ride-hail, or delivery at the time of
Disablement.

5.2 Disablement occurring during racing, off-road driving, or on non-maintained
terrain.

5.3 Disablement while the driver is operating under the influence of alcohol or
drugs.

5.4 Vehicles not registered or not licensed for road use.

5.5 Routine or scheduled maintenance, and any repair not required to make the
vehicle driveable or safe to tow.

## 6. Additional Benefits

6.1 **Trip Interruption — Rental / Taxi.** If a covered Disablement leaves the
member more than 50 miles from home and the vehicle cannot be repaired the same
day, the plan reimburses alternate transportation (taxi or rental car) up to
**$150 per Disablement**.
